About PioTech

Based at Pioneer Academy of Science in Wayne, New Jersey, USA, Team 8771 - PioTech is proud to be part of the FIRST Mid-Atlantic District. Since our rookie year in 2022, we've been dedicated to fostering innovation, teamwork, and excellence in robotics. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of engineers and technologists through competitive robotics and community engagement.
