Our School’s Legacy

Preserving over a century of excellence, tradition, and growth.

Foundations of Our Institution

Established in 1905, our school began as a single-room academy with a handful of eager students. Over the decades, we have grown into a respected institution known for academic rigor, community engagement, and steadfast values.

Timeline of Growth

1905

Our humble beginnings: the original wooden structure where dedicated teachers nurtured the first generation of students.

1920

The first graduating class: a milestone that marked the beginning of our long tradition of cultivating future leaders and innovators.

1950s & Beyond

Significant expansions and modern facilities shaped the learning environment. From dedicated science labs to a fully stocked library, our school embraced the future without losing sight of its roots.
